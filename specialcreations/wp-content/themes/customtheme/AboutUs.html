About Us
Paper Creation is a company which provides many types of personal handmade gift. We operate all around the world through this online store and well as a physical store at Sims Drive. An example of our service would be customers can pick from a wide selection of gifts they want, pick the color they prefer, and we will make the gift with those specifications in a short period of time. Paper Creations aims to help people who want to personally make a gift to their special person.
